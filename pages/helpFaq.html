General

PathwayAI is an AI-powered career guidance platform built for students and early-career professionals. We help you discover your ideal career path through personalised assessments, skill gap analysis, job matching, and AI-generated roadmaps tailored to your skills and interests.

Yes! PathwayAI offers a free tier with access to the Career Quiz, basic career matching, and limited AI roadmap generation. Premium plans unlock unlimited roadmaps, full job matching, advanced AI insights, and priority support.

Features & Career Quiz

The Career Discovery Quiz is an AI-powered assessment that analyses your interests, skills, work preferences, and personality traits. Our model then matches you with career paths and provides a compatibility percentage. You can retake the quiz anytime — results improve as you update your profile.

Absolutely. You can take the Career Discovery Quiz as many times as you like. Each attempt is saved in your Quiz History so you can track how your interests evolve over time.

The AI Roadmap generates a personalised, step-by-step career roadmap based on your quiz results and stated career goal. Each roadmap includes learning milestones, skill checkpoints, and recommended resources. You can create multiple roadmaps and track progress with completion tracking.

Billing & Account

Manage your subscription from Dashboard → Settings → Subscription Plans. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time — changes take effect at the next billing cycle. To cancel, go to your plan settings and select "Cancel Subscription."

To reset your password, click "Forgot Password" on the Sign In page — you'll receive a reset link within 2 minutes. To update your email, go to Profile → Settings → Security.

You can permanently delete your account from Profile → Settings → Security → Delete Account. This removes all your quiz history, roadmaps, and personal data within 30 days in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
