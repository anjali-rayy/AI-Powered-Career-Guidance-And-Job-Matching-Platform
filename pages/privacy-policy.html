§ 01

Introduction

PathwayAI ("PathwayAI", "we", "us", or "our") operates an AI-powered career guidance platform accessible at pathwayai.app and related subdomains (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect personal information about you when you access or use the Service.

This policy applies to all users, including visitors who browse without an account, registered account holders, and any individual whose personal data is submitted through the platform. It covers all features, including the Career Quiz, AI Roadmap Generator, 3D Career Tree, and any features we may introduce in the future.

PathwayAI is the data controller for personal data processed under this policy. By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you should discontinue use of the Service immediately.

Scope note
This policy does not cover third-party websites or services linked from our platform. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties, and we encourage you to review their respective policies.

§ 03

Information We Collect

We collect personal data in three ways: information you provide directly, information collected automatically when you use the Service, and information from third parties where applicable.

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account credentials: Your name, email address, and password. Passwords are stored only in hashed form and are never stored in plaintext.
  • Career and profile data: Educational background, work experience, career goals, self-assessed skill levels, and areas of interest entered during the Career Quiz or profile setup.
  • Communications: Messages sent to our support team, responses to surveys, and content submitted via feedback forms.
  • Newsletter subscription: Your email address, if you choose to subscribe. Subscription is entirely optional.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features accessed, button clicks, session duration, referral URLs, and navigation paths.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, screen resolution, and time zone.
  • Log data: Server-side logs recording interactions with the Service, including timestamps, error reports, and API request metadata.

3.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not knowingly collect the following categories of sensitive personal data, and our platform is not designed to receive them:

  • Racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs
  • Trade union membership, genetic data, or biometric data
  • Data concerning health, sex life, or sexual orientation
  • Government-issued identification numbers (e.g., Aadhaar, passport number, SSN)
  • Financial account numbers or payment card data
Please be aware
Do not include sensitive personal information in any text field on our platform. If such data is submitted inadvertently, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

§ 04

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable data protection law (including the GDPR) requires us to identify a legal basis for processing your personal data, we rely on one or more of the following:

  1. Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Processing necessary to provide the Service, including account management and generating career roadmaps.
  2. Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests — such as improving the platform, ensuring security, and preventing fraud — where those interests are not overridden by your rights.
  3. Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): Processing carried out with your explicit consent, such as sending marketing emails. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  4. Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): Processing necessary for us to comply with applicable laws and regulatory obligations.

If you are located outside the EEA, the above provisions are offered for transparency. We apply equivalent data protection standards regardless of your jurisdiction.

§ 05

How We Use Your Data

We use personal data only for the purposes set out below. We do not process personal data for purposes incompatible with those stated here without obtaining your prior consent or as otherwise permitted by law.

5.1 Providing and Operating the Service

  • Creating and managing your account; authenticating your identity and maintaining session security
  • Generating personalised AI career roadmaps, skill-gap analyses, and career recommendations
  • Displaying your career history, progress, and saved results

5.2 Improving and Developing the Service

5.3 Marketing and Communications (with Consent)

5.4 Security and Legal Compliance

  • Detecting, investigating, and preventing fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service; complying with applicable laws, court orders, and legal processes
  • Protecting the rights, property, and safety of PathwayAI, our users, and the public
Important limitation
We do not use your personal data to train external or third-party AI or machine learning models. We do not use your data for targeted advertising or to build advertising audience profiles. We do not make solely automated decisions about you that produce legal or similarly significant effects without human review.

§ 07

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not permit third parties to use your personal data for their own commercial purposes. We may disclose your personal data only in the limited circumstances described below.

7.1 Service Providers (Data Processors)

We engage trusted third-party vendors to process personal data on our behalf. These vendors are contractually required to: (a) process data only on our documented instructions; (b) implement appropriate security measures; and (c) not use your data for any purpose other than performing services for us. Current categories include cloud infrastructure providers, transactional email delivery services, and analytics tools.

7.2 AI Inference Providers

Career profile data you submit may be transmitted to third-party AI API providers (such as Google Gemini API) solely to generate your personalised career roadmap and recommendations. These providers are bound by contract to: (a) not use your data to train or improve their own models; (b) not disclose your data to any other party; and (c) delete inference data after processing. We do not send account credentials or sensitive personal data to AI providers.

7.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose personal data when we have a good-faith belief that disclosure is necessary to: (a) comply with a legal obligation, subpoena, or court order; (b) protect and defend our legal rights or those of our users; (c) prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing; or (d) protect the personal safety of users or the public.

7.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, personal data may be transferred to the successor entity. We will provide notice before your data becomes subject to a materially different privacy policy, and will seek your consent where required by applicable law.

7.5 With Your Consent

We may share your personal data with third parties in additional circumstances where we have obtained your explicit prior consent.

§ 10

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have some or all of the following rights with respect to your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@pathwayai.app. We will respond within 30 days, or within such other period required by applicable law.

Right of Access
Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, along with information about how it is being processed (Art. 15 GDPR).
Rectification
Request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data (Art. 16 GDPR).
Erasure
Request deletion of your personal data where it is no longer necessary, or where you withdraw consent. Subject to certain legal retention obligations (Art. 17 GDPR).
Restriction
Request that we restrict processing in certain circumstances, such as while a rectification request is being verified (Art. 18 GDPR).
Portability
Where processing is based on consent or contract, receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format and transmit it to another controller (Art. 20 GDPR).
Object
Object to processing based on our legitimate interests, or object to direct marketing at any time (Art. 21 GDPR).
Withdraw Consent
Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing. Unsubscribe from marketing emails via the link in any email.
Complain
Lodge a complaint with your relevant supervisory authority (e.g., ICO in the UK, or your national data protection authority in the EU) if you believe we have not handled your data lawfully.

We may ask you to verify your identity before processing a rights request. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights. Where legal obligations prevent us from fulfilling a request in full, we will explain the applicable limitations in our response.

§ 11

Children's Privacy

The Service is directed to users who are at least 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or process personal data from children under 13 (or under 16 where required by applicable law, including the EU GDPR and the UK Children's Code).

If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided personal data without consent, they should contact us immediately at privacy@pathwayai.app. Upon receiving verified notification, we will take reasonable steps to delete the relevant data promptly.

For parents and guardians
Parents or legal guardians of minor users (where permitted by applicable law) may contact us to review, correct, or request deletion of their child's personal data at any time.
§ 12

Security

We implement and maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure, or access. Our measures include:

  • Encryption of all data in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption of data at rest for sensitive stored data
  • One-way hashing of passwords using bcrypt with appropriate cost factors
  • Role-based access controls limiting personnel access to production data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews, vulnerability assessments, and dependency audits
  • Incident response procedures to detect, report, and address personal data breaches

No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a personal data breach likely to result in high risk to individuals, we will notify affected users and the relevant supervisory authority in accordance with applicable law.

If you discover a potential security vulnerability, please disclose it responsibly to security@pathwayai.app before making any public disclosure.

§ 13

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our data practices, the Service, applicable technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last revised" date in the document header at the top of this page
  • Send an email notification to all registered users no fewer than 14 days before the revised policy takes effect
  • Display a prominent notice on the Service for a period of not less than 30 days following the effective date

Where a proposed change involves processing your personal data in a materially different manner, we will seek your consent prior to implementing that change where required by applicable law. Your continued use of the Service after a revised policy takes effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
