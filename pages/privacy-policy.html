§ 01

Introduction

PathwayAI ("PathwayAI", "we", "us", or "our") operates an AI-powered career guidance platform accessible at pathwayai.app and related subdomains (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information about you when you access or use the Service.

This policy applies to all users — including visitors who browse without an account, registered users, and anyone whose data is submitted through the platform. It covers all features including the Career Quiz, AI Roadmap Generator, 3D Career Tree, and any features we introduce in the future.

By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the Service.

Scope note
This policy does not cover third-party websites or services linked from our platform. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third parties you visit.

§ 03

Information We Collect

We collect personal data in two ways: information you provide directly, and information collected automatically when you use the Service.

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account credentials: Your name, email address, and password. Passwords are stored only in hashed form and are never stored in plaintext.
  • Career and profile data: Educational background, career goals, self-assessed skill levels, and interests entered during the Career Quiz or profile setup.
  • Communications: Messages sent to our support team or submitted via feedback forms.
  • Newsletter subscription: Your email address, if you choose to subscribe. This is entirely optional.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features accessed, session duration, and navigation paths.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, and time zone.
  • Log data: Server-side logs recording interactions with the Service, including timestamps and error reports.

3.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, health data, government ID numbers, financial account details, or biometric data. Our platform is not designed to receive such information.

Please be aware
Do not include sensitive personal information in any text field on our platform. If such data is submitted inadvertently, contact us immediately at support@pathwayai.app so we can delete it.
§ 04

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable data protection law requires us to identify a legal basis for processing your personal data, we rely on one or more of the following:

  1. Performance of a contract: Processing necessary to provide the Service, including account management and generating career roadmaps.
  2. Legitimate interests: Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests — such as improving the platform and ensuring security — where those interests are not overridden by your rights.
  3. Consent: Processing carried out with your explicit consent, such as sending marketing emails. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  4. Legal obligation: Processing necessary for us to comply with applicable laws and regulatory obligations.
§ 05

How We Use Your Data

We use personal data only for the purposes set out below and do not process it for incompatible purposes without your consent.

5.1 Providing and Operating the Service

  • Creating and managing your account; authenticating your identity
  • Generating personalised AI career roadmaps, skill-gap analyses, and career recommendations
  • Sending transactional communications such as account confirmations and password resets

5.2 Improving the Service

  • Analysing aggregated, anonymised usage patterns to identify bugs and improvements
  • Testing new features and interfaces internally

5.3 Marketing (with Consent)

  • Sending newsletters, career tips, and product updates — only where you have opted in

5.4 Security and Legal Compliance

  • Detecting, investigating, and preventing fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service and complying with applicable laws
Important limitation
We do not use your personal data to train external or third-party AI models. We do not use your data for targeted advertising or build advertising profiles. We do not make solely automated decisions about you that produce legal effects without human review.

§ 07

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share it only in the limited circumstances described below.

7.1 Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party vendors to process personal data on our behalf — such as cloud hosting and transactional email delivery. These vendors are contractually required to process data only on our instructions and to implement appropriate security measures.

7.2 AI Inference Providers

Career profile data you submit may be transmitted to third-party AI API providers (such as Google Gemini API) solely to generate your personalised career roadmap and recommendations. These providers are bound by contract to not use your data to train or improve their own models, and to delete inference data after processing.

7.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose personal data when required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users or the public.

7.4 With Your Consent

We may share your personal data in additional circumstances where we have obtained your explicit prior consent.

§ 10

The Service is directed to users who are at least 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided personal data without consent, they should contact us at support@pathwayai.app. We will promptly delete the relevant data upon verified notification.

For parents and guardians

§ 11

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect personal data. Our measures include:

  • Encryption of all data in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • One-way hashing of passwords using bcrypt
  • Role-based access controls limiting personnel access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews and dependency audits

No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. If you discover a potential security vulnerability, please disclose it responsibly by emailing support@pathwayai.app with the subject line "Security Disclosure".
